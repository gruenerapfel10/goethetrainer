One company: many breakthroughs

At Owkin, we’ve designed a structure that maximizes both focus and collaboration. Our group of specialized companies work seamlessly together, combining deep expertise with a shared mission to accelerate biological discovery.
Owkin Core (Owkin K)

The technology foundation of our ecosystem is Owkin K. It is our cutting edge software that incorporates high quality multimodal data and AI tools to power breakthroughs across our companies.

Epkin

Developer of OKN4395, a first-in-class selective triple inhibitor of EP2, EP4, and DP1. Currently in phase 1 (INVOKE), it is the first Owkin program to fully leverage AI, from asset selection to clinical development.

Owkin Dx

A leader in clinically validated digital pathology AI diagnostics. Solutions to support pathologists, screen biomarkers, and predict patient outcomes. The product portfolio spans tools for clinical trials, and regulatory-approved IVD solutions for routine clinical use.

Bioptimus

Incubated by Owkin
Bioptimus is building the first universal reference AI foundation model in biology to drive advancements in scientific research and biotechnological innovation.

What makes our technology different?

  • World-class, AI-ready, multimodal patient data ingested and harmonized from a network of leading academic partners

  • Cutting-edge new modalities such as single-cell and spatial omics via MOSAIC
